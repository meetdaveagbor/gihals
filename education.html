About Us

GIHALS' Commitment to Promoting Formal and Informal Education

Goodness Initiative for Health Awareness and Life Sustainability (GIHALS) is a nonprofit organization dedicated to fostering sustainable development through education. Recognizing that education is the foundation of social and economic progress, GIHALS actively promotes both formal and informal education to ensure equitable access to knowledge and skills for all individuals, regardless of background, ability, or socioeconomic status

Our efforts align with global education frameworks, particularly the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 4 (SDG 4), which aims to "ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all." Through various initiatives, GIHALS empowers individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary to improve their lives and contribute meaningfully to society.

Access to Quality Formal Education

Formal education is a powerful tool for reducing inequality and fostering national development. GISHALS is committed to ensuring that children, youth, and adults, including marginalized groups, have access to quality, inclusive, and equitable formal education. .
